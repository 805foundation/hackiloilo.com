Hack Iloilo

Theme: How can we leverage technology to improve education in the Philippines?

Bring your creativity, curiosity, and passion to Iloilo's unique hackathon. A hackathon is a coding event in which teams “hack” together to create an awesome project like a game, website, or app. Team up with some of the brightest minds in Iloilo and—fueled by free food and the chance to win prizes—turn your creative energy into award-winning projects that entertain and inspire.

What is Hack Iloilo?

- This 12-hour program was designed to empower undergraduate and graduate students to experience the innovation and excitement that occurs when the power of coding is unleashed

- Our event's theme-Smart Education-prompts attendees to build projects that make education for all students a bit easier.

- Attendees will have the opportunity to network and collaborate with others.

- Lunch, dinner, and snacks are provided.

- Attendees can expect door prizes, awards and plenty of swag.

When is the event?

This 12-hour long event formally kicks off on August 21 from 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m.

Who should attend?

This event is open to undergraduate and graduate students All experience levels are welcomed!

What can I win?

- First place receives ₱25,000

- Second place receives ₱15,000

- Third place receives ₱10,000

- All participants will receive a certificate acknowledging their hard work and diligence in competing in Hack Iloilo.
